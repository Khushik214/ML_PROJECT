Transparency & Methodology

How our intelligence works, its limits, and the standards we uphold.

The Model

Our system utilizes a Classification model trained on a curated clinical dataset of over 70,000 health entries. By analyzing correlations between biometrics (BP, cholesterol) and historical outcomes, it provides an estimate of risk probability.

Limitations

While statistically accurate (84%+), this tool is a predictive algorithm based on population trends. It cannot account for individual genetic nuances, recent life events, or sudden physiological changes.

Ethical Notice: We do not store or sell your personal health metrics. All calculations are ephemeral and utilized only for the duration of your session.
