About CardioAI

Bridging the gap between clinical data and personal awareness.

Our Mission

Cardiovascular disease remains the leading cause of mortality globally. Many of these conditions are preventable if risks are identified early. CardioAI was built to demonstrate how Machine Learning can recognize subtle patterns in clinical biometrics, empowering individuals to stay informed about their well-being.

How it Helps

By transforming complex clinical datasets into an accessible, intuitive assessment tool, we help users visualize their health metrics through the lens of advanced prediction algorithms.
