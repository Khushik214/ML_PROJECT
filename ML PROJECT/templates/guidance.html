Health Guidance

Sustainable lifestyle tips and awareness for a stronger heart.

Active Habits

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity each week. Consistency is the key to cardiovascular resilience.

Nutrition Focus

Prioritize leafy greens, whole grains, and lean proteins. Reducing sodium and trans fats is a direct path to healthier blood pressure levels.

Stress Management

Chronic stress impacts heart health. Incorporate meditation, deep breathing, or restful sleep routines into your daily schedule.
